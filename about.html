Damián Hernández is a Chilean digital animator in his final year at Universidad Mayor. His strongest skills are in 3D animation, color design (for colorscripts and color exploration), compositing, background painting, video editing, and the ability to adapt to different artistic styles. He also has basic knowledge and experience in storyboarding, layout, cleanup, general production, art direction, and general direction. Damián thrives in collaborative environments, bringing a proactive attitude and strong communication skills to every team. His commitment to creative excellence and adaptability allow him to consistently deliver high-quality results across diverse projects. He has an upper-intermediate level of English, holding a TOEFL certification at the B2 level.
